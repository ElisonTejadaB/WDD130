Solutions and Services:

We are customer service oriented, and we also have a high level of technical expertise. Our team is a group of tech-savvy professionals who focus on solving tricky technical issues. All our personal have a degree in a tech-related field and relevant certifications which are required to be a support specialist. We have more than 20 years of experience offering solutions to customers.

    We provide three types of service:
  • Local service desks are tied to a specific organization and are usually located at or near their office.
  • Centralized service desks serve a large region and several different clients. They tend to be their own entity instead of being tied to a specific business.
  • Virtual service desks employ remote staff across multiple locations and do not have a physical location. This is currently the most common model.
Here is a list of the main services we offer:

Technical Support:

Find a solution to the problem of the same, whether physical (hardware) or logical (software) of any type of electronic device.

Identifying malfunctioning components like RAM, hard drives, graphics cards, etc., through diagnostic tools and visual inspection. Replacement and Installation: Replacing faulty components with new ones, ensuring proper compatibility and functionality.

Server Installation:

Select the server hardware, and operating system, choose a good location, configure it, and implement security. Server Administration: Manage and secure company data and information.

Identify or Diagnose Problems:

Process of identifying, analyzing, and resolving issues and errors in the computer system.

Server Maintenance:

Keeping server software updated and running smoothly so that a computer network functions properly and avoids downtime or loss of data.

Application Developments:

Creating a computer program or a set of programs to perform the different tasks that a business requires.

Troubleshooting Problems:

Define the problem, collect relevant information, analyze collected data, propose a solution, and test it. Finally, implement the solution.
